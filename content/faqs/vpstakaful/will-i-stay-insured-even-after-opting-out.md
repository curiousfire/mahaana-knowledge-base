---
question: Will I stay insured under VPS Takaful even after opting out?
answer: >
  Yes, your existing coverage does not end immediately when you opt out. Each
  deposit you made keeps your coverage active for 12 months from that deposit
  date. So your coverage gradually reduces as each deposit reaches its 12-month
  anniversary, and fully ends when the last eligible deposit expires. You will
  receive an email notification before each reduction and when your coverage
  ends completely.
