---
question: How to file a claim?
answer: >
  In the unfortunate event of a customer's death, their registered beneficiary
  can file a claim with IGI Life. Please note that a 90-day cooling-off period
  applies from your activation date — no claim is payable for any cause of death
  during this period.


  Step 1: Notify IGI Life within 30 days of the death.


  Step 2: Gather the required documents:

  - Completed Claimant Statement form

  - Original Death Certificate issued by Union Council / Municipality / Cantonment Board

  - Copy of the deceased's CNIC (attested)

  - Copy of the insurance application / agreement form

  - If death occurred at home — an Affidavit attested by the area Union Council

  - Investment balance details for the past 6 months


  Step 3: Submit to IGI Life Claims Department: 7th Floor, The Forum, Suite No. 701-713, G-20, Block-9, Khayaban-e-Jami, Clifton, Karachi-75600.


  Step 4: Settlement within 15 business days once all documents are received. The benefit — 2× your total eligible investment, capped at PKR 10,000,000 — is paid to your registered beneficiary.
