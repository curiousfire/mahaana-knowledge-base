question: How does this work exactly?
answer: >
  Every deposit you make into your Mahaana VPS account automatically builds your
  life insurance coverage. Each deposit gives you coverage equal to 2× your
  deposit amount for 12 months from that deposit date. The more you deposit and
  the more regularly you deposit, the higher your coverage. A small 2% fee
  (called FEL) is included in each deposit — no separate payment is needed. Your
  coverage is reviewed and activated by our team, after which a 90-day
  cooling-off period applies for new members.
