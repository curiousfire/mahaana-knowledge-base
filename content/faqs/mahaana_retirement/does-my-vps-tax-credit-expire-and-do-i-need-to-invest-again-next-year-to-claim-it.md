question: Does my VPS tax credit expire, and do I need to invest again next year
  to claim it?
answer: >-
  Yes, the VPS tax credit works on a per-financial-year basis. The credit you
  claim for a given tax year is tied to the investment you made in that year. It
  does not carry forward.

  Every financial year (July to June) is a fresh slate. Whatever you invested this year earned you a tax credit for this year's return. But once that year closes, the credit is consumed. Next year, to claim the same benefit, you need to invest again.

  The good news is that your money stays invested and keeps growing. The investment itself does not expire or get withdrawn. Only the tax credit is a one-year benefit. So to keep claiming the credit year after year, you simply need to keep contributing fresh amounts each financial year.

  To directly answer the question, yes, invest again next year if you want the credit again.
